Why Study at FKSSS?

FKSSS is positioning itself to be a leader in offering courses geared towards producing bright and not just students in areas outlined by the Vision 2030.



Fr.Kemper Sisokhe Secondary set in a welcoming and accessible forested environment, 1hr 30 min drive from Kakamega, is far enough to offer serene studying environment but close enough for convenience.



We offer market sensitive Studies tailored to careers in Physics, Biology, Chemistry, Business,Computer,Agriculture,Mathematics,Languages (Kiswahili and English),Geography,History and Christian Religious Education.
